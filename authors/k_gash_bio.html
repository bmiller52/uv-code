BIO

Hey there! I help around with the editing and clarity of the work that gets done here. It’s my job to help make the website reader-friendly and the writing pleasing to the eye. As a writer myself, this comes easily to me. I began coding in the September of 2016 when I enrolled in a computer science class at my co-op with my fellow coders Brett and Luke. Having been a beginner to coding, it was an entirely new experience for me, and I soon found that I enjoyed the designer aspect of it—making websites using HTML and presenting them in a clear way that appeals to the users.

I’ve been interested in writing and editing for years. As a sixteen-year-old trying to balance many different aspects to my high school life, my standards for writing aren’t professional or especially unique. However, I have a great level of appreciation and respect for literature, and personally enjoy the challenge of executing a clear, sharp style in my writing that efficiently communicates any given concept. With the exception of several ongoing novels and the editing of this website, this literary pastime mostly comes out in the form of artistic or poetic writing.

I appreciate many other forms of art as well, such as painting, drawing, and photography, but my heart lies the most in musical art. As a dedicated pianist for nearly five years, I have a deep passion for the piano and most classical music. Many varieties of music appeal to me in addition to this: a major genre I’ve been listening to recently is Indie Rock.

Happy coding!
